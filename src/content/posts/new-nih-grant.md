We are thrilled to announce that MIRL has received a new R01 grant from the National Institutes of Health (NIH) to support our research on deep learning for accelerated MRI reconstruction.

## Grant Details

- **Title**: Physics-Informed Deep Learning for Clinical MRI Acceleration
- **Funding**: $2.5M over 5 years
- **Agency**: NIH/NIBIB
- **PI**: Dr. Sarah Chen

## Research Goals

This grant will support the development of:

1. Novel physics-informed neural network architectures
2. Self-supervised learning methods that don't require fully-sampled training data
3. Clinical validation studies across multiple MRI applications
